What you need to do next

Gather all the information listed here then enter details of each employer and the jobs they want to add. 

Always give the employer’s Companies House (CH) registration number, if there is one. This helps us to check and process your variation.

Tell us the employer’s Charity Commission or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number if they do not have a CH number.

We will ask for:

  • your Kickstart Scheme Application ID. You can find this on your grant agreement or on payment remittance documents
  • each employer’s registered name and registered address
  • each employer’s Companies House (CH) registration number, if they have one
  • their Charity Commission or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number, if they have one but no CH number
  • the number of jobs each employer is offering as part of this variation and where they are based
  • details abut how you, as a gateway, check that all jobs offered meet the Kickstart Scheme criteria

If you wish to offer Kickstart Scheme jobs within your gateway organisation add your details in the same way as for other employers.
