What you need to do next

Gather all the information listed here then enter details of each employer and the jobs they want to add. 

We use Companies House records to assess your variation. We can pull information held by Companies House to help complete the online application and save you time.

If the employer is a charity without a Companies House number tell us their Charity Commission or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number, if they have one.

We will ask for:

  • each employer’s registered name and registered address
  • each employer’s Companies House registration number, if they have one
  • their Charity Commission or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number, if they have one but no Companies House number
  • the number of jobs each employer is offering as part of this variation and where they are based
  • details about how you, as a gateway, check that all jobs offered meet the Kickstart Scheme criteria

If you wish to offer Kickstart Scheme jobs within your gateway organisation add your details in the same way as for other employers.
