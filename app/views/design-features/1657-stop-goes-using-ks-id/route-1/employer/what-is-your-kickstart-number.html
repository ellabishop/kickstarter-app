What is your Kickstart Scheme Application ID?

If you are an employer who has applied through a Kickstart gateway in the past:

  • do not use your gateway’s Kickstart Scheme Application ID in this online service
  • you can contact your gateway to discuss adding more Kickstart Scheme jobs

You can find your Kickstart Scheme Application ID on your grant agreement from DWP or on payment remittance documents. It starts with KS followed by 8 characters. For example: KS123ABC78.
