What is your Kickstart Scheme Application ID?

You can find your Kickstart Scheme Application ID on your grant agreement or on payment remittance documents.

If you are an employer who has applied through a Kickstart gateway, you cannot use your gateway’s Kickstart Scheme Application ID in this online service.
Contact your gateway to discuss additional Kickstart Scheme jobs.
It starts with KS followed by 8 characters. For example: KS123ABC78.
