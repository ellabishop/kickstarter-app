Getting young people into the Kickstart Scheme jobs

Changes to your existing Kickstart Scheme jobs that you need to tell us about and how to create more Kickstart Scheme jobs.

Employer guidance

Kickstart Scheme applications close at midday on 17 December 2021.

Check the other deadlines for completing Kickstart Scheme tasks.

  • Submit your vacancies

If you're a Kickstart gateway, check the guidance for gateways.

Before you can get a young person into a job

You need to:

  • successfully apply online or apply through a Kickstart gateway for a Kickstart Scheme grant before midday on 17 December 2021
  • sign and return the grant agreement to DWP or your Kickstart gateway before 11:59pm on 7 January 2022
  • submit your vacancies by 11:59pm on 31 January 2022

How to get the young people into the jobs

The total grant is made up of:

  • Work coaches at Jobcentre Plus will use the vacancies you or your gateway submitted to match candidates to the jobs. They’ll do this until 1 March 2022.

  • You or your Kickstart gateway will receive a ‘referral’ email each time a young person is referred to the vacancy.

  • Referred candidates may choose to apply for the job placement.

  • You’ll then be able to interview the candidates who have applied.

  • The young person must start the job on or before 31 March 2022.

  • You or your Kickstart gateway should tell DWP by 11:59pm on 30 April 2022 that the young person has started so we can process the funding. You’ll be told how to do this in the ‘referral’ email.

You can advertise vacancies yourself, but all jobs must receive an introduction through a DWP work coach to receive full funding. DWP also uploads the approved vacancies to Find a job on your behalf – so you do not have to do this. Young people can search this site for Kickstart vacancies themselves. We’ll advertise Kickstart jobs on Find a job until 28 February 2022.

If a young person leaves the job early

You must email DWP as soon as possible if a young person either:

  • leaves their job before the end of the 6-month period
  • needs to temporarily leave their job (for example for special leave or COVID-19-related restrictions)

The email address is the same one you use to tell DWP about the young person’s start date. You’ll be told how to do this in the ‘referral’ email you get when a work coach refers a young person to your job vacancy.

If a young person leaves their job early, the next scheduled wage payment from DWP will be the last you receive for them.

DWP may be able to extend the funding period if the young person has to temporarily stop working.

You can replace the person who left early with another young person referred to the vacancy by a work coach. They must start on or before 31 March 2022.

Add more jobs to your grant agreement (grant variation)

You can add more jobs to your grant agreement after it has been approved. This is known as a ‘grant variation’.

The additional jobs will be assessed to make sure they meet the Kickstart Scheme criteria.

If you applied directly, you can add more jobs online using the application service (until midday on 17 December 2021).

If you applied through a Kickstart gateway, tell them you’d like to add more jobs. They will contact DWP to arrange this and must do this before midday on 17 December 2021.

Get help and support with managing your Kickstart Scheme jobs

Contact your local or national Kickstart Scheme contact if you need help with the application process.
