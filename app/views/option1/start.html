Apply for a Kickstart Scheme Grant for Employers

Use this service to apply for a Kickstart Scheme employer grant. You can use this service if you are:

  • an employer and have 30 or more Kickstart scheme job placements
  • representing a group of employers (Kickstart gateway)

If you can't offer more than 29 Kickstart scheme placements you can find a Kickstart gateway such as a local authority, charity or trade body for help applying

Start now

Before you start

You will need: the Companies House reference number, Charity Commission number or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number, if you have one the organisation address and contact details details of the Kickstart Scheme job placements and their location supporting information to show that the job placements are new jobs and meet the Kickstart Scheme criteria information about the support the organisation can give to develop employability skills of young people

If you are applying as a Kickstart gateway you will need these details for each organisation you are applying on behalf of.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

You cannot register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.
