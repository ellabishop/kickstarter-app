Apply through a Kickstart gateway

If your organisation is looking to create between 1 and 29 Kickstart Scheme job placements you need to follow a different process.

You can:

  • choose a Kickstart gateway to help you apply for the grant. Some may be able to provide employability support on your behalf. You will need to give them information about the job placements you want to create
  • contact your local Jobcentre Employer Partnership if you need help to decide what to do
